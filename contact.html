Find answers to common questions about Terro AI and our platform.

Our platform uses advanced LiDAR and photogrammetry techniques combined with AI processing to create highly accurate 3D models of real-world environments. These models are then optimized for robotics simulation, allowing you to train and test robots in virtual replicas of actual spaces with sub-centimeter precision.

Terro AI integrates seamlessly with major robotics frameworks including ROS (Robot Operating System), ROS2, Gazebo, Isaac Sim, and popular game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine. Our API also allows custom integrations with proprietary systems.

Mapping time depends on the size and complexity of the environment. A typical warehouse floor (50,000 sq ft) can be scanned in 4-8 hours and processed into a simulation-ready model within 24-48 hours. Our team handles the entire process, from scanning to delivery.

Yes! Our platform supports incremental updates. You can add new areas, modify existing layouts, or update specific elements without rescanning the entire environment. We also offer scheduled remapping services for facilities that change frequently.

We offer comprehensive support including dedicated technical account managers, 24/7 email support for enterprise customers, extensive documentation, API reference guides, and regular training webinars. Our team is committed to ensuring your success with Terro AI.

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR regulations. For enterprise customers, we offer on-premise deployment options and custom security configurations.
