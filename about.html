About Terro AI

Bridging physical and digital worlds to accelerate the future of robotics.

Our Mission

To create the most accurate 3D representations of real-world environments, enabling robots to understand and navigate physical spaces before ever encountering them. We're making robotics development faster, safer, and more accessible.

Our Vision

A world where robots seamlessly operate in any environment through perfect digital twins. Where the gap between simulation and reality is virtually zero, and AI-powered machines enhance human capabilities everywhere.

Our Journey

2020

Founded

Terro AI was founded by a team of robotics researchers and computer vision experts with a vision to revolutionize how robots perceive the world.

2021

First Product Launch

Released our core 3D mapping SDK, enabling developers to create accurate virtual environments from real-world scans.

2022

Series A Funding

Raised $25M to expand our platform capabilities and grow our team of world-class engineers.

2023

Enterprise Platform

Launched enterprise-grade solutions, partnering with Fortune 500 companies in manufacturing and logistics.

2024

Global Expansion

Opened offices in Europe and Asia, serving customers across 30+ countries with localized support.
