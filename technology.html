Our Technology

An end-to-end platform for converting physical spaces into simulation-ready 3D environments with unparalleled accuracy.

Core Technology

3D Scanning & Reconstruction

Multi-sensor capture system supporting LiDAR, RGB cameras, depth sensors, and structured light. Our proprietary algorithms process point clouds into dense, accurate meshes in real-time.

AI/ML Pipeline

Deep learning models for scene understanding, object recognition, and semantic segmentation. Neural networks trained on millions of environments for robust performance.

Simulation Engine

Physics-accurate virtual environment generation with realistic lighting, materials, and dynamics. Compatible with major robotics simulators and game engines.

Integration APIs

RESTful APIs and SDKs for Python, C++, and JavaScript. Seamless integration with ROS, Gazebo, NVIDIA Isaac, and Unity/Unreal Engine.

Technical Deep Dive

Our mapping process begins with multi-sensor data fusion, combining LiDAR point clouds with high-resolution imagery for comprehensive coverage. The system automatically aligns data streams using SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithms, achieving sub-centimeter accuracy in most environments.

Post-processing includes mesh generation, texture mapping, and automatic LOD (Level of Detail) creation for optimal performance across different use cases.

Our AI pipeline uses transformer-based architectures for scene understanding, trained on proprietary datasets of over 10 million labeled environments. Models perform real-time semantic segmentation, object detection, and material classification.

Continuous learning from deployed systems improves accuracy over time, with federated learning ensuring privacy while aggregating improvements.

Generated environments include accurate physics properties derived from material classification. Friction coefficients, mass distributions, and collision meshes are automatically generated for realistic robot interaction.

Our simulation engine supports rigid body dynamics, soft body physics, and fluid simulation for comprehensive testing scenarios.

Flexible deployment options include cloud processing for maximum compute power, edge deployment for low-latency applications, and hybrid configurations. All deployments include automatic scaling based on workload.

Enterprise customers can deploy on-premises using our containerized solutions, maintaining full data sovereignty while leveraging our platform capabilities.
